Top 5 Tourism Places in Karnataka

Discover Nature, Heritage & Beauty

Coorg

Known as the Scotland of India, Coorg offers lush greenery, coffee plantations, waterfalls, and a peaceful environment.

Hampi

A UNESCO site filled with ancient temples and ruins of Vijayanagar empire. A paradise for history lovers.

Jog Falls

One of India’s highest waterfalls, offering breathtaking views especially during monsoon.

Mysore Palace

A royal architectural marvel showcasing Karnataka’s heritage and grandeur.

Kabini

Famous for wildlife safaris, forests, and serene nature experiences.
