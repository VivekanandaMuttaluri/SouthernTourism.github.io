New Travel Batches

We start a new travel batch every month on the 1st. Each batch is a 15-day journey exploring the beauty of South India, traveling comfortably by bus.

Discover breathtaking destinations, vibrant cultures, and serene landscapes. Join us to make unforgettable memories!

Things to Carry

Category Items
Travel Documents
  • Valid ID proof (passport, Aadhar card, etc.)
  • Travel tickets (bus, train, flight)
  • Hotel or tour booking confirmations
  • Itinerary with addresses and emergency contact numbers
  • Copies of documents (physical and digital)
Clothing
  • 5-7 sets of comfortable clothes (lightweight and appropriate for the weather)
  • Innerwear and socks (at least 7-10 pairs)
  • Sleepwear
  • A lightweight jacket or sweater
  • Comfortable walking shoes and flip-flops
  • A scarf, hat, or cap (for sun protection)
  • Raincoat or umbrella (seasonal)
Toiletries
  • Toothbrush, toothpaste, and floss
  • Soap, shampoo, and conditioner (travel-sized bottles)
  • Deodorant or perfume
  • Sunscreen and lip balm
  • Comb or hairbrush
  • Hand sanitizer and wet wipes
  • Razor and shaving essentials (if needed)
  • Feminine hygiene products (if applicable)
Health Essentials
  • Prescription medicines (if any) and a doctor’s note
  • Basic first-aid kit (band-aids, antiseptic, painkillers, etc.)
  • Vitamins or supplements
  • Insect repellent
Electronics
  • Mobile phone with charger
  • Power bank
  • Travel adapter (if required)
  • Earphones or headphones
  • Camera and extra batteries/memory cards (optional)
Money and Cards
  • Sufficient cash in local currency
  • Debit/credit cards
  • Travel wallet to organize documents and money
