Top 5 Tourism Places in Andhra Pradesh

Explore the beauty of South India

Amaravathi

The Amaravathi Buddha statue stands as a symbol of peace and enlightenment. Located near Krishna River, it reflects rich Buddhist heritage and attracts visitors worldwide.

Prakasam Barrage

A stunning engineering marvel offering scenic beauty with calm waters and lush greenery. A perfect destination for nature lovers and photographers.

Tirumala

A sacred hill town known for Sri Venkateswara Temple. Surrounded by lush hills, it attracts millions of devotees every year.

Visakhapatnam Beaches

Golden sands, cool breeze, and beautiful sunsets make Vizag beaches a perfect getaway for relaxation and adventure.

Araku Valley

A scenic hill station with coffee plantations, waterfalls, and misty mountains. A paradise for nature lovers.
